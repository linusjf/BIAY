### BIAY Day 177 By Cynthia Dsilva

Hosea 11:1

\*Out of Egypt I called my son.\*

Literally, God was referring to the old Israel as His son.
Israel was redeemed from idolatry and physical bondage under the Pharaoh in Egypt.

Prophetically, God was referring to Jesus, the new Israel (Matthew 2:15) and us.

Jesus was brought home to Nazareth from Egypt after the death of King Herod.

God wants each one of us, His children, to come out of our Egypt (spiritual bondage due to attachment to the world) and come home to Him (surrender).
